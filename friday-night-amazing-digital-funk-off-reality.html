Unlock The Digital Beat In Game

Friday Night Amazing Digital: Funk Off Reality opens the door to a strange world. The powerful rhythm draws you into a digital reality. Inspired by The Amazing Digital Circus, this mod pushes your experience into a virtual space with a series of unique characters.

Lost In Digital Music

The story begins when Boyfriend picks up an old pair of virtual reality glasses. After a second of wearing the glasses, you will be teleported to a strange space that no one has ever set foot in. Here, Boyfriend transforms into a flexible robot, while Girlfriend transforms into a lively version of Bonbon & Chuchu, two characters coming out of cartoon dreams.

The adventure begins with a rap battle with Caine on a vibrant remix. But that's just the beginning! The next challenges against Jax, Pomni, and Ragatha will force you to show off your amazing musical sense.

More than just music, Funk Off Reality turns each level into a visual feast with magical digital effects. Every note you hit gives you the feeling of carving your own personal mark into this virtual world.

Fight Against Powerful Opponents Through Special Moves

Your mission is to help Boyfriend defeat his opponents through exciting rap battles. Players use the navigation keys to perfectly synchronize with the rhythm of the song. Arrows will appear on the screen, and you need to hit them at the right time to score points and maintain the performance. Don't let the enchanting melodies fool you - each song increases in speed and difficulty, forcing you to concentrate.
